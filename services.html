Services

Personalized training solutions for every fitness level

As a NASM certified personal trainer, I offer comprehensive fitness solutions tailored to your individual needs and goals. Whether you're just starting your fitness journey or looking to push your limits, I provide the guidance, support, and expertise you need to succeed.

Explore my service offerings below and discover the perfect fit for your fitness journey.

1:1 Personal Training

Personalized one-on-one sessions tailored to your specific goals, fitness level, and preferences. This is the most direct and effective way to achieve your fitness goals with dedicated attention and customized programming.

What's Included:

  • Initial Assessment

    Comprehensive fitness evaluation to establish your baseline and set realistic goals

  • Custom Workout Plans

    Tailored exercise programs designed specifically for your body and goals

  • Form Correction

    Real-time technique guidance to ensure safety and maximize results

  • Nutrition Guidance

    Basic nutritional advice to complement your workout program

  • Progress Tracking

    Regular assessments to monitor improvements and adjust your program accordingly

Available Formats:

In-Person Remote

In-person sessions available in the Seattle area. Remote sessions conducted via video call with real-time guidance.

Group Training

Small group sessions that combine personal attention with the motivation of training with others. Perfect for those who thrive in a social environment while still wanting personalized coaching.

What's Included:

  • Small Groups

    Limited to 2-4 people to ensure quality coaching and attention

  • Varied Workout Styles

    Experience different training methods from strength training to HIIT

  • Peer Motivation

    Draw energy and inspiration from training alongside others

  • Cost-Effective Option

    Get professional coaching at a lower price point than 1:1 training

Available Formats:

In-Person Remote

In-person group sessions available in the Seattle area. Remote sessions conducted via group video calls with real-time guidance.

Online Coaching

Comprehensive online coaching with custom plans, regular check-ins, and ongoing support. Ideal for self-motivated individuals who want expert guidance with flexible scheduling.

What's Included:

  • Weekly Plan Updates

    Receive new or updated workout plans each week based on your progress and feedback

  • Video Form Analysis

    Submit videos of your workouts for detailed technique feedback

  • Regular Check-ins

    Scheduled video calls to discuss progress, address concerns, and adjust your program

  • 24/7 Support

    Direct messaging access for questions and guidance between scheduled check-ins

  • Nutrition Tracking

    Guidance on tracking and optimizing your nutrition to support your fitness goals

Available Format:

Remote

All online coaching is conducted remotely through a dedicated coaching app and video calls.
